Simon Nyokabi Njuguna

Pursuing: Environmental Science & Technology

His Story

Simon has always been deeply passionate about ecological conservation, environmental science, and sustainable habitat management. Growing up, he witnessed the challenges faced by local ecosystems and wanted to study scientific methods to address them.

Through the Maisha Madrugada Foundation's full sponsorship, Simon is now studying Environmental Science at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). He is gaining the research and data-gathering skills required to help design sustainable habitat preservation solutions and lead local conservation awareness programs.

Vincent Githinji Muthoni

Pursuing: Information Technology

His Story

Vincent believes that information technology is a powerful tool to solve complex operational challenges and connect communities. Supported by the Maisha Madrugada Foundation since 2014, he has successfully advanced his educational journey.

Vincent is currently in his third year of studying Information Technology at Jomo Kenyatta University of Agriculture and Technology (JKUAT). He plans to use his training to build databases, tracking systems, and digital platforms that help local conservation groups organize information and expand their community outreach.

Claire Kiende

Pursuing: Clinical Counselling Psychology

Her Story

Claire is dedicated to promoting mental health wellness and supporting emotional resilience among youth and families. Recognizing the lack of accessible clinical counseling in many local communities, she decided to study counselling therapy.

With the foundation's full sponsorship, Claire is studying Clinical Counselling Psychology at Outspan Medical College. Her goal is to establish community counseling networks, run mental wellness workshops, and provide essential mental health care to individuals in need.
